Within the past month, Walmart security has received specific information regarding fraudulent coupons being passed at their store.     On December 22, 2013, Craig Tucker was detained by Walmart security as he matched the description and he had just passed 2 fraudulent coupons for Monster energy drink and Arizona Ice Tea beverages while paying for other items.
